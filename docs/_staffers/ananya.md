Hello! My name is Ananya G M, and I'm one of your TAs this summer! I'm a rising 3rd-year PhD student at UC Berkeley EECS, advised by [Amy Pavel](https://amypavel.com/). My research focuses on designing new interactions for accessible computer use to improve human-AI collaboration. Specifically, I study differences in interaction styles among sighted users, blind users, and computer use agents, with the goal of reducing gaps in strategy, time, and effort. I completed my Master's in Human-Computer Interaction from the School of Information at UT Austin, then spent another year and a half in a PhD program at UTCS before moving to UC Berkeley with my amazing lab!

In my free time, I go on backpacking trips, cook all kinds of food, and jam with friends to Indian music. Excited to be working with y'all this summer!
